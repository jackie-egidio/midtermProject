What are the ticket prices? What is the slowest time of day? Can you bring outside food?
Please refer to our booking page for pricing information. We tend to be busier around lunchtime, so come in the evening or morning if you prefer the quiet. Yes! We have picnics outside for the whole family to enjoy a meal.
Are there lockers available? Can adults participate? Is there an age requirement for any of the activities?
Programmable lockers are available free of charge for guests to store their personal items in. Of course! Our activities are made for the whole family. There is no age requirement for mini golf. You have to be 5 years old or more for the trampoline park and 11 years old or more for the rest of the attractions.
May I have a refund? Who cannot participate in the attractions? What should I wear?
You may have a refund up to 24 hours before your scheduled booking. Our trampoline and go karting attractions are not meant for those who:
  • Have recently had surgery

  • Have neck or back injuries

  • Are pregnant
 Wear comfortable clothes like sneakers and workout clothes, as some of our activities are strenuous.
